# Database Coverage

Database version: **7**  
Stored records: **104**

## Included in this release

- 35 named islands and settlements
- 11 forts
- 7 dive or shipwreck locations
- 4 smuggler dens
- 12 Ultimate Ship Plans
- 4 legendary encounters
- all 26 documented Resynced buried-treasure dig locations
- 3 July 2026 community treasure-hunt chests
- Blackbeard’s Treasure side quest
- Exotic Sea Shells trinket

Every stored record uses the existing verification system. The new treasure, quest and trinket records are marked **Resynced verified** because their coordinates and details were checked against current July 2026 guides.

## Save compatibility

The storage key remains `acbf-companion-m3`, and the user-data version remains 3. Existing location IDs were not renamed. The two treasure records already present in v3.1.3 were updated in place rather than duplicated.

## Next regional audit phase

Ordinary money chests, Secrets/art pieces, viewpoints, huntable animals, Data Files, individual shanties and other local collectibles are not yet complete across every island. These should be added region by region from Resynced-specific collectible guides rather than inferred from the 2013 map.
